# Introduction
SpritVale is an Indie-MMORPG inspired by Ragnarok Online. It's currently in PlayTest. You can choose between the start classes Knight, Warrior, Mage, Summoner and Ranger.

## Game Mechanics
### Dodge Roll
By Pressing **Space** you can perform a dodge roll that can be used to avoid enemy's attacks.
The dodge will give you a 0.5 second invulnerability so that you can even dodge targeted skills.

### Waypoints
Throughout the world you'll find waypoints. You can unlock them by clicking on them.
Once unlocked, you will be able to teleport to them from any other waypoint.

### Waystone
If you talk to the Waybinder NPC start location for the first time, he will give you a **Waystone**
that can be used to teleport back to Nevaris. The item will not be consumed.

### Storage
West of the Waypoint in Nevaris you'll find a NPC that will give you access to your storage.
You can deposit items with a combined weight of up to 10,000 inside your storage. The storage
is shared across all characters on your account.

### Card Removal
South-west of the Waypoint in Nevaris is a NPC that will allow you to remove
cards from your gear.

### Equipment Upgrading
North of the Waypoint in Nevaris is a NPC that will allow you to upgrade your equipment
and artifacts.
Upgrading your gear will increase the base stats of your equipment (Atk for weapons and Def
for armor). The equipment and cards will give additional *per refine* stats that will
also scale with the upgrade level of the equipment.

Upgrading requires additional materials are dropped by monsters but can also be bought
at the NPC next to the Blacksmith.

Upgrading gear can fail. Higher level gears have a lower success chance. Failing the
upgrade will decrease the level by one. It's possible to use a save upgrade method
that won't decrease the level in case of failure using higher quality materials.

### Boss Monsters
Many maps have boss monsters. These monsters are harder to kill and have special loot
and higher drop chances for regular loot. When a boss monster is killed, a tomb stone
appears stating when and by whom the boss was killed. They have a respawn cooldown
of one hour. Each boss has an item that can be used to summon them that is dropped
by monsters on the same map.
